Blizzard's Content Release Plan

Blizzard wants to recreate the content release similar to the original Vanilla WoW. They announced that the patch level will be at version 1.12 (all talents, items, class mechanics etc.) and content will be released in six phases. In a separate post they outlined when PvP content will be released. Combined this results in the following release plan:

  1. Launch: MC, Ony, Maraudon, Thorium Point quest hub, original mount system (cheap training, expensive mounts), NO unarmored epic mounts
  2. DM, Kazzak, Azuregos, Honor System and PvP Rank Rewards. Layering is said to be disabled by Phase 2
  3. BWL, DMF and Darkmoon Decks, cross-realm BGs starting with 1.12 AV and WSG
  4. ZG, Green Dragons, AB
  5. AQ + war effort, Silithus content, T0.5, Relics, drop rate and item drop location changes
  6. Scourge Invasion, Naxx, world PvP objectives in Silithus and EPL

This has an impact on what items are available/BiS at any given time and will be considered here.

Phase 1

  • Complete MC and Onyxia attunements!
  • If you intend to tank Ragnaros in MC, prepare Fire Resistance gear: dark-iron-helm, dark-iron-leggings, dark-iron-gauntlets, dark-iron-bracers (dark-iron-boots are NOT recommended due to RNG stats and exalted reputation requirement), enchant lesser-arcanum-of-resilience on head/legs, scout the AH for blazing-emblem
  • Start preparing 360 [Thorium Widget] (1080 [Thorium Bar] + 360 [Runecloth]) or 480 [Dense Grinding Stone] (1920 [Dense Stone] ) for [Amulet of the Darkmoon] (Phase 3)
  • Key tank items from dungeons: naglering, force-of-will, draconian-deflector
  • Key tank items from raids: onyxia-blood-talisman,
  • Focus on getting your epic mount before Phase 2 hits!

Phase 2

  • Get at least Rank 2 in the Honor System for insignia-of-the-alliance and insignia-of-the-horde respectively
  • New entry level item options from DM (East, West, North): satyrs-bow (if you lack +hit), force-imbued-gauntlets, fel-hardened-bracers, eldritch-reinforced-legplates, timeworn-mace (esp. if Human tank), bulky-iron-spaulders, hedgecutter (esp. if Orc tank), kromcrushs-chestplate, redoubt-cloak, tarnished-elven-ring (if you absolutely need +hit)
  • If your guild is competing for world bosses (Kazzak, Azuregos), some item options: empyrean-demolisher, puissant-cape, unmelting-ice-girdle (handy for Naxx in P6), eskhandars-left-claw
