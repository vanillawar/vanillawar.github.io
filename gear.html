Blizzard's Content Release Plan

Blizzard wants to recreate the content release similar to the original Vanilla WoW. They announced that the patch level will be at version 1.12 and content will be released in six stages:

  1. Launch: MC, Ony, Maraudon
  2. DM, Kazzak, Azuregos
  3. BWL, DMF and Darkmoon Decks
  4. ZG, Green Dragons
  5. AQ, T0.5, Silithus content
  6. Scourge Invasion, Naxx

This has an impact on what items are available/BiS at any given time and will be considered here
