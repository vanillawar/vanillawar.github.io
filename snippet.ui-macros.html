UI and Macros

Keep in mind, that WoW Classic uses the modern WoW client API!
Most 1.12 macros/addons relying on scripted targeting or spell casting will not work!

Addons

  • Must have addons: equipment manager, enemy health in percent, some auction/mail addon
  • Must have addons for PvE: threat meter, boss mods, Cthun warner
  • Must have addons for PvP: enemy cast bar, spell warner

Macros 1.13.* (Classic)

Start attack: 

#showtooltip
/startattack
/cast Mortal Strike

Charge/Intercept + Hamstring: 

#showtooltip
/startattack
/cast [stance:1] Charge; [stance:3] Intercept
/cast Hamstring

One button ranged: 

/cast Throw
/cast Shoot Gun
/cast Shoot Crossbow
/cast Shoot Bow

Weapon equip macros (2H, DW, 1H+Shield, slot 16 is MH, slot 17 is OH): 

/stopcasting
/equip Bonereaver's Edge
/stopcasting
/equipslot 16 Tooth of Eranikus
/equipslot 17 Serathil
/stopcasting
/equipslot 16 Tooth of Eranikus
/equipslot 17 Aegis of Stormwind

Macros 1.12 (Vanilla)

Use upper/lower trinket: 

/script UseInventoryItem(13);
/script UseInventoryItem(14);

Reset instances: 

/script ResetInstances()

KEYBIND EVERYTHING!
