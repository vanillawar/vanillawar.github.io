To get your rank and progress ingame, you may want to install the HonorSpy addon or execute the following macro:

/run _,r=GetPVPRankInfo(UnitPVPRank("player"),"player");r=r+GetPVPRankProgress();rs=string.format("%.4f",r);DEFAULT_CHAT_FRAME:AddMessage("PvP Rank: "..rs)

Tips:

  • Take a screenshot of your honor tab before and after honor update reset!
  • While the game does not tell (last week's) pool size, you may infer it from your progress and standing by guessing and adjusting pool size numbers in the above calculator until it fits.
  • HonorSpy's pool size and bracket estimation can and will never be accurate because it can't track all players in the pool.

Estimation, honor stacking and bracket slots

The estimation does not take into account honor stacking and assumes linear distribution of honor contribution inside a bracket (which would happen naturally without stacking). The calculator can still be used to estimate your progress if you are stacking. To do so, change your standing to the standing you are honor stacking on in your bracket (e.g. if there are 10 bracket 1 slots, and the top 9 players stack on standing 1's honor, the top 9 players may change their standing to standing 1 for calculation/estimation purposes). The projection is to be taken with a grain of salt, since usually the number of players and your standing are not constant. You are however free to manually estimate and enter your RP gains, the calculated result is then accurate.

In order for stackers to get the maximum amount of RP out of their bracket, the last standing of each bracket should stack with the next lower bracket. So, brackets span sufficient amounts of honor and breakpoints always fall on the honor of stackers. This effectively reduces the number of slots in bracket 1 by one.

SoM

This blue post explained SoM honor system changes:

  • RP gain is essentially doubled.
  • The example they've given with 6 weeks of st1 to r14 wouldn't work with 20% decay (then it'd be just 3 weeks). However, the example works with 40% decay. So it's safe to assume, they doubled decay, too.
  • Whether negative delta is still capped at -2500 ("you won't ever lose more than half a rank"), we'll see. They may have doubled that as well, so it might be -5000 ("you won't ever lose more than a rank").

Q: "I got bracket 1 in first SoM update, your calculator said I'd get r7, but I'm only r3?! What the?!"

A: There are RP (rank) caps per level. So if you got some HKs in SoM a few hours before the first honor update, you likely weren't on a high level. Below level 30, you are capped at 6500 RP total (rank 3.30). The calculator does not take your level into account and does not consider RP limits per level.

Some SoM servers launched a few hours before the first honor update. Players who took advantage of this and PvP'd in lvl10-19 WSG may progress like this (st1 capped to r3.3 first update and getting full RP the later weeks). Overall, still a minimum of six honor updates are needed to reach r14 from r0.

Update 2021-12-03: SoM Honor System Bugs

Looks like small indie company messed up again. And is working on a fix.

To me, this looks like they either didn't double RP gain the first week(s) or forgot to increase decay alongside the 2x RP gain. Either way, their honor system didn't behave as advertised. I added another option to the calculator which might explain players having too fast progress (2x RP gain, 20% decay). That would result in 3 weeks of st1 needed to go from r0 to r14, which is probably not intended, kek!

Maybe should have not used beta for streamer promo, but to actually test things?

Update 2021-12-04: SoM Honor System Fixed

SoM honor system progress seems to be fixed and recalculated for all players now (2x RP gain, 40% decay). The SoM honor calculator proves accurate, again.
