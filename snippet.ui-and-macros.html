Keep in mind, that WoW Classic uses the modern WoW client API, so most 1.12 macros/add-ons relying on scripted targeting or spell casting will not work!

UI

  • Have keybindings!
  • Must have add-ons: equipment manager, enemy health in percent, some auction/mail add-on
  • Must have add-ons for PvE: threat meter, boss mods, Cthun warner
  • Must have add-ons for PvP: enemy cast bar/spell warner

Macros

Start attack: 

#showtooltip
/startattack
/cast Mortal Strike

Charge + Hamstring/Intercept: 

#showtooltip
/startattack
/cast [stance:1] Charge; [stance:3] Intercept
/cast Hamstring

One button ranged (not confirmed, yet!): 

/cast Throw
/cast Shoot Gun
/cast Shoot Crossbow
/cast Shoot Bow

Use upper/lower trinket (not confirmed, yet!): 

/script UseInventoryItem(13);
/script UseInventoryItem(14);

Reset instances (not confirmed, yet!): 

/script ResetInstances()
