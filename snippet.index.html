Why play a Warrior in WoW Classic?

  • Key class, valid for any PvP objective, highest DPS, only real tank in WoW Classic
  • Rewarding gear progression, epic melee combat experience
  • mortal-strike, hamstring, charge, intercept etc.
  • Unstoppable juggernaut if supported well

Selecting Faction and Race

Alliance

  • blessing-of-freedom, blessing-of-kings and blessing-of-salvation
  • Do not need to fight blessing-of-freedom and blessing-of-protection. No divine-shield + Hearthstone
  • fear-ward
  • Access to the exclusive Alchemy recipe restorative-potion
  • Lower entry level for successful raiding
Human
  • Best DPS/TPS due sword/mace skill
  • diplomacy yields a gear advantage in early game
Dwarf
  • stoneform enables you to prey on rogues, trivializes WSG flag extraction against rogue defense
  • stoneform is another defensive CD for progression tanking, helps in various instances, raids and while leveling
Gnome
  • escape-artist covers a warrior's greatest weakness (competent frost mages will still destroy you)
Nightelf
  • shadowmeld allows for unique PvP tactics, spell dodging, getting more Charge openers
  • shadowmeld can also be used as "poor man's" stealth in combination with invisibility potions
  • wisp-spirit grants an advantage in world PvP draws, despite being mocked a lot

Horde

  • windfury-totem
  • Not getting grilled by endgame Elemental Shamans, no Paladins stealing your plate loot
  • Access to exclusive items like horned-viking-helmet, rune-of-the-guard-captain and nat-pagles-extreme-angler-fc-5000
  • Higher instance clearing speed potential for organized raids/groups
Orc
  • blood-fury and axe skill, best DPS, best OT TPS (for taunt immune bosses)
  • hardiness is the best Horde PvP racial for Warriors
Tauren
  • war-stomp as additional interrupt, shutting down enemy avoidance
  • Bigger melee range and hitbox
  • Additional health helps for progression, but Vanilla is figured out
Undead
  • will-of-the-forsaken helps with fear-heavy bosses, reduces damage taken in PvP because you can fight in Battle Stance more
  • will-of-the-forsaken allows escaping Succubus/magic-dust
  • cannibalize as another bandage
Troll
  • Best for TPS tanking
  • Overshadowed by other Horde races

TL;DR:

Alliance: Human (PvE) or Gnome/Dwarf (PvP)
Horde: Orc (female) or Orc (male)

Keep in mind that you need to be comfortable with the animation/looks of your character! My picks: Dwarf for Alliance, Orc for Horde!
