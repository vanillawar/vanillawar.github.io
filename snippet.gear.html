Blizzard's Content Release Plan

Blizzard wants to recreate the content release similar to the original Vanilla WoW. At BlizzCon 2018 they announced that the patch level will be at version 1.12 and content will be released in four stages:

  1. MC, Ony, DM, Kazzak, Azuregos
  2. BWL, BGs and PvP rewards, ZG, (DMF was not mentioned, but would also fit here)
  3. AQ, T0.5, Silithus content, Green Dragons
  4. Scourge Invasion, Naxx

This has an impact on what items are available/BiS at any given time and will be considered here
