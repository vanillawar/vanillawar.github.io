Professions

At level 60, Engineering is a must for Vanilla. Options for the 2nd trade skill:

  • Mining (solo play, very compatible with Engineering)
  • Blacksmithing (Armor for guild progression, Weapon for anti-fear trinket)
  • Enchanting (disenchant in your raids/guild, make money sitting in capitals)
  • Herbalism (farm your own consumables)

Professions def. not to take or to have on alts:

  • Alchemy (make your own pots, lvl35 alt is good enough, own Arcanite/transmute timer)
  • Enchanting for disenchant only (in Vanilla you can disenchant any item already at an enchanting skill of 1)
