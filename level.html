Vanilla Warrior Leveling Guide

Talents

31/20/0 Arms is best for leveling. It provides damage value on every tier compared to Fury, which only yields Cruelty (5% crit) on the 1st tier and has a drought till the 4th tier (Enrage). Arms also includes Tactical Mastery which is great for tanking in dungeons and world PvP.

First 31 points into Arms:

  1. 3/3 Improved Rend
  2. 2/5 Deflection
  3. 2/2 Improved Charge
  4. 3/5 Tactical Mastery
  5. 2/2 Improved Overpower
  6. 3/3 Deep Wounds
  7. 2/2 Impale
  8. 2/5 Two-Handed Weapon Specialization
  9. 4/5 Tactical Mastery
  10. 1/1 Sweeping Strikes
  11. 5/5 Axe Specialization
  12. 3/3 Improved Hamstring
  13. 5/5 Tactical Mastery (best for kiting hard mobs)
  14. 1/1 Mortal Strike

Then the last 20 points into Fury:

  1. 5/5 Cruelty
  2. 5/5 Booming Voice
  3. 1/1 Piercing Howl
  4. 3/3 Blood Craze
  5. 1/5 Improved Battle Shout
  6. 5/5 Enrage

Axe spec is always preferred while leveling, even if you do not always have an axe. You have some freedom with the Tactical Mastery, Two-Handed Weapon Specialization and Improved Hamstring talents. If you do not (need to) kite or you play on a PvE server, you may want to skip Improved Hamstring completely. Always use your best weapons, it does not matter whether that is a 2-hand or 2 1-h weapons!

Finally 60! What now?

  • Do BRD, then progess to 5mans like Strat, Scholo, DM, LBRS, eventually do UBRS
  • Get [Skull of Impending Doom]
  • Get [Nifty Stopwatch]
  • Get [Horned Viking Helmet] (Horde only)

You may also want to improve your gear, make some gold and optimize your supply chain.
