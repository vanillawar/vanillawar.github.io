Casual Guild

Scope:

  • Farm MC/BWL/20mans on one raid day, get people geared for PvP
  • AQ40/Naxx are unrealistic with one-raid-day policy and should only be pursued if the previous instances can be phased out due to having a very stable guild (unlikely)
  • Queues on high-pop servers force people to login early during the day. Thus making best raid times for such guilds: Saturday or Sunday morning/midday

Phase 1: MC + Ony

Key objective: get guild core to 60, attuned and setup for MC + Ony, get epics, push for epic mount

Professions

  • Enchanting: 1-2 guild enchanters
  • Alchemy: greater-fire-protection-potion, maybe flasks (buying Black Lotus from AH due to high competition)
  • Skinning: for scale-of-onyxia, core-leather
  • (optional, could also ask HC guild) Armorsmith: FR gear
  • (optional, could also ask HC guild) Leatherworking: onyxia-scale-cloak, FR gear
  • (optional, could also ask HC guild) Mining: for dark-iron-bar, elementium-bar
  • (minmax) Goblin Engineer: goblin-sapper-charge

Quests

  • UBRS key: starts with handing in unadorned-seal-of-ascension at vaelan
  • MC water/dousing quest line (required for Ragnaros): chain starts with stormers-and-rumblers and poisoned-water
  • MC attunement: attunement-to-the-core
  • Ony attunement: dragonkin-menace (Alliance), warlords-command (Horde)
