PvP

  • Have binds (all skills, all consumables, all gear sets)
  • Learn to flank, stay ahead of flag carriers
  • Remember and use your unique utility: Mortal Strike, Hamstring/PH, Intercept+Charge, Disarm, Taunt and RNG weapon procs for insta-gib™

Useful consumables

  • [Swiftness Potion]
  • [Major Healing Potion] (or any other healing potion)
  • [Whipper Root Tuber]
  • [Noggenfogger Elixir]
  • [Iron Grenade] or [Thorium Grenade]
  • [Goblin Sapper Charge]
  • [Powerful Anti-Venom] (you should not need to use it that often, otherwise it can get expensive)

  • [Mighty Rage Potion] (expensive due to requiring Gromsblood)
  • [Restorative Potion] (expensive due to requiring Elemental Earth, Alliance only recipe)
  • [Living Action Potion]
