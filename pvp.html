PvP

The Warrior class has a very reactive playstyle. You act in the error of your opponent and your burst is mostly RNG based (crits, sword spec procs). Due to this, it is often improper for Warrior players to take ownership of successful 1v1 outcomes.

On the other hand, the rage mechanic is forgiving errors and your abilities are effective with minimal effort. This allows you to focus more on macro play (positioning, coordinated efforts, BG objectives) as (outwitting your opponents in 1v1 situations)

Basics

  • Have proper keybinds (all skills, all consumables, all gear sets, all on-use items)
  • Learn to flank, stay ahead of flag carriers
  • Learn to peel
  • Remember and use your unique utility: Mortal Strike, Hamstring/PH, Intercept+Charge, Disarm, Taunt and RNG weapon procs for insta-gib™
  • Have various gear sets (offensive PvP set, defensive PvP set, tank/maximum survival set) and consumables ready

Classes

Warriors are generally suboptimal for (fair) duels. However, in real world PvP the situation can be in your favor (e.g. getting the opener/Charge, enemy having used powerful cooldowns already). The pointers below will also help in BGs, since lots of encounters there could be seen as a chain of unfair 1v1 or 1vX situations.

Warlock

  • If Destruction + Succubus: RIP
  • Save Intercept for after Death Coil
  • Shadow Reflector helps
  • Being Undead (WotF) helps

Priest

  • Shadow Priest: RIP
  • Other specs: hope for RNG, align big hitters with RNG
  • Mortal Strike helps, so does Death Wish
  • When you pop Recklessness, save the Pummel for Mind Control

Hunter

  • The better your gear gets, the clearer is your advantage
  • 1H + Shield while being kited, 2H when you closed the distance
  • Use LoS
  • Frost Reflector helps with trap, so does mocking/taunting the pet

Shaman

  • Elemental: RIP
  • Try to reflect Frost Shock

Paladin

  • Reckbombs are not in your favor, hit and run might work
  • Shockadins or Holy+Reck melt your HP in no time, avoid
  • In general: you do not need to fight, just run to your closest base, stay mounted if you are mounted already, mount when the Paladin mounts. Not much they can do about it, use /lol macro

Warrior

  • RNG fest
  • Have weapon chain(s)
  • Fight with 1H + Shield, defensive playstyle wins here
  • Setup Intercept, switch to 2H and unload all your rage
